About the league

What is IWEL

The International Wizarding Esports League is a global, fan run competition framework for Quidditch Champions. IWEL focuses on clear documentation, transparent governance and a safe, competitive environment where teams represent their nations on an international stage.

Global structure

Teams compete while representing a national banner. Formats, seeding rules and tiebreakers are documented up front so captains know exactly what they are playing for.

Written governance

Decision making lives in public documents. Leadership roles, committees and rule change procedures are laid out in writing instead of handled behind closed doors.

Player safety

A Code of Conduct, enforcement guidelines and appeals process define how IWEL handles reports and protects players, staff and the wider community.
