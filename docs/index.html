IWEL Season 00

Founders Season

Player sign ups are now open. Season 00 is a structured, low pressure test run that builds the national rosters for IWEL Season 1.

Key dates

Important dates for Season 00

Season 00 is designed so players can commit early, get evaluated fairly and avoid clashing with League 7 sign ups and schedules.

League sign ups end
December 21, 2025
Season 00 begins
January 3, 2026

Players register individually and list their top three countries they would like to represent. Season 00 runs for six weekends across three tryout cycles. Each cycle has:

  • One weekend of tryouts with temporary teams
  • One week of new sign ups and late registrations
  • One week of planning, squad building and schedule prep

Players can be evaluated up to three times, once for each of their nation preferences.

Season 00

Official timeline

Below is the full Season 00 structure from the first tryout weekend through the Continental Showcase and the transition into IWEL Season 1.

Cycle 1 - Nation Preference 1

Weekend 1: January 3 to 4 - Tryout Weekend 1

Players appear under their Nation Choice 1. Temporary teams are formed and captains begin evaluation.

  • 4 round matches
  • Ranking system: +3 win / +1 tie / +0 loss
  • Captains scout and take notes

Signups Week 1 - Open registration (Jan 5 to 9)

  • Late sign ups allowed
  • New players choose their three nations
  • Player list finalized at 11:59 pm on January 9

Planning Week 1 (Jan 10 to 16)

  • Assign players to new tryout squads
  • Build match schedule for Weekend 2
  • Captains review notes and scouting
  • Staff confirm lobby hosts, streamers and refs
  • Players rest

Cycle 2 - Nation Preference 2

Weekend 2: January 17 to 18 - Tryout Weekend 2

Players show up for Nation Choice 2 on new squads. Captains look for growth and consistency.

  • 4 round matches
  • Focus on improvement since Cycle 1

Signups Week 2 - Open registration (Jan 19 to 23)

  • Final chance for brand new players to join
  • Anyone who missed Cycle 1 can enter here
  • Sign ups list closes end of day January 23

Planning Week 2 (Jan 24 to 30)

  • Organize squads for Tryout Weekend 3
  • Captains prepare scouting templates
  • Nations refine draft boards
  • Staff finalize stream schedule and referees

Cycle 3 - Nation Preference 3

Weekend 3: January 31 to February 1 - Tryout Weekend 3

Players appear for Nation Choice 3. This is the final evaluation cycle.

  • 4 round matches
  • Last chance for captains to see the full talent pool

Signups Week 3 - Final open registration (Feb 2 to 6)

  • For players who still want to join IWEL Season 1
  • Missed all three tryout cycles
  • Evaluated separately via scrims, interviews or VOD review

Planning Week 3 (Feb 7 to 13)

  • Nations finalize draft boards for IWEL Season 1
  • Captains rank players based on tryout performance
  • Staff prepare Season 1 materials and announcements
  • Teams begin soft branding and flag or crest approvals

Season 00 finale and transition

Feb 14 to 15 - Continental Showcase Weekend (optional)

A final showmatch weekend where nations field preliminary rosters.

  • Captains test team chemistry
  • Streamed matches build hype
  • Commentary introduces each nation identity
  • No rankings or penalties - pure showcase

Feb 16 to March 1 - Season 00 wrap up

  • Nations submit final Season 1 roster lists
  • Staff finalize Season 1 schedules, graphics and any rule updates
  • Optional formal draft week
  • League 7 likely runs its season, so IWEL pauses official matches
  • Once L7 stabilizes, IWEL Season 1 begins with fully prepared rosters

The goal of Season 00 is to enter Season 1 fully ready: scouted players, established national identities, a warmed up player base and zero competition with League 7 sign ups.
